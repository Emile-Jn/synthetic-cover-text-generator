`imdb_reviews.txt` is the file `data/adg/cover.txt` in the repo of the paper *Towards Next-Generation Steganalysis: LLMs
Unleash the Power of Detecting Steganography* by Bai et al. (2024).  
Available [here](https://github.com/ba0z1/Linguistic-Steganalysis-with-LLMs).

`stanford_imdb.txt` is obtained by running `python3 -m src.download_dataset --sort asc --output-filename stanford_imdb.txt`
